Cool Not Cooling Enough

Is your air conditioner not cooling enough? This is a common issue that can be caused by various factors. Below are some possible reasons why your AC might not be cooling effectively:

  • Dirty or clogged air filter
  • Low refrigerant levels
  • Faulty thermostat
  • Blocked air vents
  • Broken compressor
  • Improper AC size for the room
  • Leaking ducts
  • Electrical issues
  • Frozen evaporator coils
  • Old or worn-out AC unit

If you're experiencing any of these issues, it might be time for a service check!

Power Supply Issues / AC Not Getting Power

If your AC is not turning on or you are experiencing power supply issues, several factors could be causing the problem. Below are some possible reasons:

  • Power outage or disruption in electrical supply
  • Tripped circuit breaker
  • Faulty power cord or plug
  • Loose connections in the power supply
  • Damaged or blown fuse
  • Defective internal wiring in the AC unit
  • Incorrect voltage supply
  • Malfunctioning capacitor
  • Electrical surge or fluctuation
  • Failed compressor or motor

If you're facing any of these issues, it’s advisable to get your AC checked by a professional technician to ensure safe and efficient operation.
